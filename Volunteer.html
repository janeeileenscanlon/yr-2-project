Paws of Hope

Join Our Team!
Interested in joining our team? Well you're in luck! We're always looking for energetic, open and kind people to add to our roster of volunteers. You can expect to meet many friendly new faces, furry and otherwise. Our volunteer program provides complementary training in dog behaviour and good practice for a professional pet environment. It looks great on a CV even if you're not looking for work within animal care and is a fantastic experience nonetheless.
An ideal candidate at Paws of Hope should:

-be open to new experiences and people

-love dogs and animals

-have lots of energy!

-have lots of patience!!

-be willing to get their hands dirty

-have a good attitude
Anyone interested can contact us with a CV and a cover letter at our email address pawsofhope@gmail.com. Experience is not necessary but it will help us to know the background you are coming from and your level of training professional or otherwise. Alternatively, you can come to us at our branch in limerick and hand in your CV personally. We'd love to see you and whoever's on the door will gladly answer any questions you might have at the time regarding volunteering or otherwise
