How it started

Hello, My name is Lily Blossom Bloom and from a very young age i have always had a passion for flowers from a young age. Arranging bouquets for gifts on birthdays and annaversaried, my close friends and family, then... I decided to move to thecity and open up very own flower shop

Founding stages

Founded in 2023, Bloom flower shop is a small local business that focuses on the ethical and sustainable production of beautiful bouquets and flower arrangments for every event in ones life, from weddings to , baby showers to funerals, Bloom flower shop is the place to get the personality and colour to any of your major life events

Our team is a diverse group of flower enthusiasts, united by our love for our so important petal pals and commitment to their fresh smell and look. From our experienced arrangers and flower growth team to our loyal customers and board members, each team member plays a crucial role in making Bloom a place of hope and second chances.
