Paws of hope, how we began.

Paws of hope. Welcome to our website. Here I hope to tell you a bit about how we began. We established back in 2018, because we are a passionate team of animal lovers who believe every animal deserves a happy, healthy life. Here we hope to create a safe place dedicated to providing care, love, and a home for animals in needs. At Paws of Hope, we focus on rescuing abandoned, neglected, and homeless pets, offering them a warm and nurturing environment. Our mission is to find a forever home for each of our furry friends, so they can thrive as part of a family.

What we do; Rescue and inhabilitation; We provide electrical care, shelter, and alot of love to animals who need it most Adoption services. Community outreach; We aim to reduce the number of homeless animals in our community. Through education and advocacy. we promote responsible pet ownership and work to reduce the number of homeless animals in our community.

Founding stages

Founded in 2018, Paws of hope began wtih a simple yet profund goal: to give every animal a chance at a happy, healthy life. What started as a small group of passionate animal lovers has grown into a fully-fledged shelter, thanks to the support of our community abandoned and the tireless efforts of our volunteers.

Our team is a diverse group of animal enthusiasts, united by our love for pets and commitment to their well-being. From our experienced veterinarins and animal care staff ton our dedicated volunteers and board members, each team member plays a crucial role in making Paws of hope a place of hope and second chances.
