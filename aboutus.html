About Us

How It Started

Hello, my name is Lily Blossom Bloom and from a very young age I’ve always had a passion for flowers. Arranging bouquets for birthdays and anniversaries for my close friends and family brought me joy. Eventually, I decided to move to the city and open up my very own flower shop.

Founding Stages

Founded in 2023, Bloom Flower Shop is a small local business that focuses on the ethical and sustainable production of beautiful bouquets and floral arrangements for every event in life—from weddings and baby showers to funerals. Bloom is the place to bring personality and color to your most meaningful moments.

Our team is a diverse group of flower enthusiasts, united by our love for our precious petal pals and our commitment to freshness and beauty. From experienced arrangers and growers to our loyal customers and creative collaborators, every person plays a role in making Bloom a place of joy, care, and connection.
